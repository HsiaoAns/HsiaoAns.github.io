The struggles of power

Posted on 9/24/18

All you avid gamers out there know that in many online games, there's often times that one set of gear you gotta work and pay for, or achievements that you need to get through. Well currently, I've started working towards an infamous title that requires you to kill the boss of a dungeon 800 times. 800 times! With my already limited amount of time, the whole process will take about 2 months assuming I can properly fill the daily quota of 10 runs per weekday and 20 runs per weekend day. This title has nice effects but I'm going to be spending 7 hours of my life on this.....
